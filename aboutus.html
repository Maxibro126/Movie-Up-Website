About Us!

This is Movie Up! A website about making movie reviews on your favourite movies with a wide range of movies and more releases of movies to come.

I'm Max Gardiner and I wanted to make this website for the people who love movies like I do and want to have their opinions on the movies that they love and the movies that they dislike or think are just mediocre at best. My passion for movies and TV series are always shown within me and I love to share my opinions as I would love to hear about yours too!
